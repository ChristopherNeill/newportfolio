Purpose of Application

Design-A-Date is a tool designed to plan a good time with ease and less indecisiveness. This application will populate the nearest cinemas and restaurants by zip code.

User story

  • AS a date seeker
  • I WANT to plan my evenings with convenience
  • SO there is less friction in making plans

APIs used

  • MovieDB
  • Gracenote
  • Zomato

How it works

Enter your zip code.
>
Once you enter your zip code, a list of cinemas will populate underneath the movie posters.
Click on any cinema and a list of resturants will populate.
